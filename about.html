Thanks for stopping by! I guess you want to hear about me now so here you go.

I’m a user experience designer based in the San Fransisco Bay Area. I enjoys the relationship between technology, research, and design. I have purple hair, I'm not afraid to open the terminal and I don't wear cool glasses. I design brands, mobile apps, websites and I can draw a pretty sweet dragon.

In the past, I've had the opportunity to work for a lot of excellent companies. A couple of them being, the Seattle Gay News where I designed reading experiences for their worldwide reader base, and Design Imports where I designed custom product for companies like Spirit Halloween, Wegmans, and Cracker Barrel.

I am wide-spanned in my T-shaped skill sets with specialized in user experience. I like taking on challenges in projects and I find solving UX problems extremely exciting.
